Cardio Classes

Spinning or Cycling

High-intensity cycling workouts that elevate your heart rate and improve cardiovascular fitness.

Zumba

Dance-based aerobics set to music, offering a fun and energetic way to stay fit and improve coordination.

Kickboxing or Cardio Boxing

Cardiovascular workouts with martial arts elements, combining punches, kicks, and aerobic exercise for a full-body workout.

HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training)

HIIT classes involve short bursts of intense exercise followed by periods of rest or lower-intensity exercise.
These classes are designed to maximize calorie burn and improve cardiovascular fitness.
