Strength Exercises

Deadlifts:

Description: Lifting a barbell from the ground to a standing position, targeting the back, hips, and legs.

Benefits: Full-body engagement, emphasizing posterior chain muscles.

Squats:

Description: Lowering the body into a seated position and then returning to a standing position.

Benefits: Strengthens the quadriceps, hamstrings, and glutes.

Bench Press:

Description:Pressing a barbell away from the chest while lying on a weight bench.

Benefits:Targets the chest, shoulders, and triceps.

Pull-Ups:

Description: Lifting the body upward by pulling up on a horizontal bar.

Benefits: Strengthens the upper back, shoulders, and arms.
